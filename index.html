Dayton Schuh

My first loves were Super Mario 64, Super Smash Bros. and Rampage. By the time I got my first computer, I was in middle school and MapleStory was all the rage. I can't begin to tell you how many hours I sunk into that game. I mean, I would wake up at 3 am for better chances to get into party quests. It was bad. I was addicted to the friendly competition of strength and the overwhelming sense of community. Thus, MapleStory was the game that solidified my future.

I took my first courses for programming and animation during that summer. It's been many years since then and I've been exposed to so many other interesting avenues through my studies. Now, I've graduated from California State University Fullerton with a bachelor's degree in computer science and I'm open to learning anything and everything; but I'll never forget what led me here.
